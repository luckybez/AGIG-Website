- **Correction:** **Approved and implemented July 17:** retire all acronym-based operating-system labels. Public copy now leads with “a private Intelligence Augmentation system built around your world,” identifies private cognitive infrastructure as what Avant Garde builds, and reserves artificial intelligence for a subordinate mechanism.

### P0.2 Restore a complete causal link from fragmented tools to human consequence

- **Evidence:** Current homepage strongly explains source fragments and maintained records, but removed the original weight examples, principles, advisory perspectives, and named-stakes proof.
- **Correction:** Add one concise hinge naming how rationale, trust, commitments, and relationships decay across people and time. Add a compact accountability recognition line without replacing the current hero.
- **Exact page/section:** Homepage continuity section and transition into private cognitive infrastructure.
- **Impact:** Copy and minor layout.

### P0.3 Establish credible, consent-safe proof

- **Travis approval required:** **Yes — service scope and duration must be commercially accurate.**
